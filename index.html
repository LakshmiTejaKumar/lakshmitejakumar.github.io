Situation

Sales at the store had stagnated for multiple years despite consistent customer footfall. Inventory wastage, especially vegetables, was increasing due to overstocking.

Task

Identify the root cause of stagnant sales, reduce dumping costs, and improve overall store profitability using available operational data.

Action

  • Extracted sales and customer data from Oracle and SAP systems and built an Excel-based dashboard using advanced Excel and Pivot Tables.
  • Defined KPIs such as monthly and daily sales, footfall count, bill value per customer, frequent customers, and customer location radius.

  • Used purchase frequency analysis to optimize indent planning, especially for perishable items like vegetables.
  • Reduced over-ordering and dumping costs, and redirected savings into targeted advertisements and offers to attract customers outside the 2 km radius.

Result

  • Significant reduction in dumping costs for perishable goods.
  • Improved inventory efficiency and indent accuracy.
  • Increase in sales through targeted promotions and offers.
  • Store layout and analysis approach continued to be used as a reference model.
